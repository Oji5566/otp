Your codes, your device
A simple two-factor authenticator that lives entirely in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
🛡️
RFC 6238 TOTP & RFC 4226 HOTP
Plus Steam Guard. All math runs in your browser with the Web Crypto API.
📦
Bring your own accounts
Paste otpauth:// URIs, scan QR codes, or import from 2FAS, Aegis, Bitwarden, and Google Authenticator.
💾
Backup any time
Export your accounts as JSON or otpauth URIs from Settings.
⚠️
A few things to know
This is a tool for personal use. Please read before you add your first account.
🗄️
Stored in this browser
Accounts live in localStorage on this device. Clearing site data, switching browsers, or using private mode will lose them — export a backup first.
🔑
Keep a second factor
Save your provider's recovery codes somewhere safe so you're never locked out.
⏰
Your clock matters
TOTP depends on accurate device time. If codes don't work, check that your system clock is correct.
